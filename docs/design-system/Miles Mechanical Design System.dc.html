A single bold M for Miles Mechanical, split into two temperatures — a cool blue stroke for air conditioning and a warm ember stroke for heating. Set in a squared badge that reads sturdy and engineered. It locks up with an Archivo wordmark and the "AC & Heating" descriptor.

02 — Colour

Two temperatures

Miles Blue is the brand — calm, trustworthy, the cooling side. Ember is the warm counterpart for heating moments and key calls-to-action. Steel neutrals carry most of every page; colour is the seasoning, not the meal. Alarm red is reserved exclusively for emergency service.

Always-visible click-to-call

On mobile, a two-button bar sticks to the bottom of every page: Call now (alarm red, highest priority) and Request service (Miles Blue). The phone number is the single reconciled NAP number used site-wide. Shown here at true iPhone width.

  • Minimum 48px tap targets — comfortably above the accessibility floor.
  • Red is reserved for the call action so it always reads as the urgent path.
  • Bar never covers form submit buttons — it tucks away on the contact page.
  • The whole screen leads with type, rating and credentials — no photo needed.
